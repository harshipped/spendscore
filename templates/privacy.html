Privacy
Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling search and filtering. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app; these providers are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and may not use it for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the app. You can also review our Terms and Conditions.
